ABOUT ANAND-ICE

Anand International College of Engineering (Anand-ICE), established in 2010 and approved by AICTE, is a leading RTU-affiliated engineering institution in Jaipur. The institute offers B.Tech programs in Civil Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence).

Anand-ICE provides a world-class academic environment with modern infrastructure, advanced laboratories, and a lush green campus that promotes innovation, learning, and research.

Recognized among the best emerging engineering institutions by The Times of India, Anand-ICE is supported by eminent academicians, scientists, and industry professionals who guide students towards academic and professional excellence.

With strong collaborations with reputed industries and research organizations, the institute ensures hands-on learning and prepares students to meet global challenges. The success of its alumni worldwide reflects Anand-ICE’s commitment to quality education.

About Rajasthan Ganita Parishad (RGP)


Rajasthan Ganita Parishad (RGP) was established in 1987 to promote teaching and research in Mathematics in Rajasthan, with its headquarters at S.P.C. Government College, Ajmer (NAAC “A” Grade).

Since its inception, RGP has been actively organizing annual conferences, invited lectures, symposiums, and research paper presentation sessions across the state, providing a strong platform for academic exchange.

RGP publishes a half-yearly research journal titled “Ganita Sandesh” and also organizes the College Level Mathematics Talent Hunt (CMTR) every year on National Mathematics Day (22 December) to encourage young mathematical talent.

To promote excellence in Mathematics, RGP awards Gold and Silver Medals, certificates, and other prizes during its annual conferences, motivating students and researchers to pursue higher achievements in the field.
