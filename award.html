To recognize and celebrate excellence in research, innovation, and academic contribution, ICINSA-2026 will confer a set of prestigious awards during the Valedictory Session of the conference. üåü Young Researcher Award

The Young Researcher Award honors an outstanding early-career researcher (typically below 35 years of age) who has demonstrated exceptional promise through innovative research contributions in mathematical sciences, nonlinear science, systems science, computational intelligence, applied mathematics, and related interdisciplinary areas.

Award Benefits:

  • Young Researcher Award Certificate

  • Cash Prize (if applicable by the organizing committee)

üèÖ Best Paper Award

The Best Paper Award is presented to the author(s) of the most impactful, original, and methodologically strong paper accepted at ICINSA-2026.

Best Session Papers will be nominated for the Best Paper Award.

Award Benefits:

  • Best Paper Award Certificate
  • Memento / Trophy
  • Opportunity to submit an extended version to a partner Scopus-indexed journal or special issue
  • Featured announcement on the conference website and proceedings
  • Cash Prize (if applicable by the organizing committee)

üé§ Best Oral Presentation Award

This award recognizes the participant who delivers the most effective and engaging presentation during the technical sessions.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Clarity and organization
  • Quality of slides
  • Time management
  • Response to questions

Eligibility

  • Only accepted oral presentations
  • In case of multiple authors, the presenter will be considered

Award Benefits:

  • Best Oral Presentation Certificate
  • Memento / Trophy
  • Recognition on the conference website and social media
  • Priority consideration for invited talk in next ICINSA edition (subject to approval)

üéì Best Student Presenter Award

This award acknowledges outstanding performance by student presenters who demonstrate strong research quality and effective communication skills.

Eligibility

  • Open to Undergraduate and Postgraduate students only

Award Benefits:

  • Best Student Presenter Certificate
  • Memento / Medal / Trophy
  • Feature in the conference newsletter
  • Discounted or complimentary registration for ICINSA-2026 (Student Category)
  • Potential nomination for Young Researcher Award in future editions
