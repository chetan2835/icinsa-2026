Awards

To acknowledge and celebrate excellence in research and academic contribution, the conference presents a set of prestigious awards. Through these awards, we aim to encourage researchers at all stages of their careers and promote a culture of high-quality, meaningful scientific engagement.

Each award will be presented under two categories (except Best Student Presenter Award):

  • Under-Graduate / Post-Graduate Student
  • Research Scholar / Faculty

The following awards will be presented in the valedictory session of the conference:

1. Young Researcher Award

This award honors an outstanding young academic or researcher (typically below 35 years of age) who has demonstrated exceptional promise through innovative research contributions in mathematical sciences, computational intelligence, applied mathematics, or related interdisciplinary fields. The recipient should exhibit originality, early-career leadership, and strong potential for future impact.

If an author wishes to be considered for the Young Researcher Award, they should indicate the same by writing "PAPER FOR THE YOUNG RESEARCHER AWARD" in the abstract at the time of registration.

Selected papers will be evaluated by an expert committee based on originality, technical quality, clarity of presentation, and relevance to the conference themes.

Perks for Awardee:

  • Official Young Researcher Award Certificate
  • Memento / Trophy
  • Cash Prize (if applicable by the organizing committee)
  • Special recognition during the Valedictory Ceremony
  • Complimentary conference registration for ICINSA-2027 (subject to approval)
  • Opportunity to publish an extended version in a special issue or partner journal

2. Best Paper Award

This award is presented to the author(s) of the most impactful, original, and methodologically strong research paper accepted for ICINSA-2026. The winning paper will demonstrate novelty, scientific rigor, and significant contribution to theory and/or application.

Papers selected as Best Session Papers will be nominated for consideration to the Best Paper Award.

Perks for Awardee:

  • Best Paper Award Certificate
  • Memento / Trophy
  • Opportunity to submit an extended version to a partner Scopus-indexed journal or special issue
  • Highlighted announcement on the conference website and proceedings
  • Cash Prize (if applicable)

3. Best Oral Presentation Award

This award recognizes the participant who delivers the most engaging, well-structured, and impactful oral presentation during the technical sessions. Evaluation includes communication skills, clarity, slide design, time management, and response to questions.

  • Only participants with accepted oral presentations are eligible
  • In case of multiple authors, the presenter will be considered

Perks for Awardee:

  • Best Oral Presentation Certificate
  • Memento / Trophy
  • Acknowledgment on the conference website and social media
  • Priority consideration for invited talk in next edition (subject to approval)

4. Best Student Presenter Award

This award acknowledges excellence among student presenters who demonstrate outstanding research quality, clarity, and enthusiasm. It encourages young scholars to pursue high-quality research and professional communication skills.

  • Only Under-Graduate and Post-Graduate students are eligible

Perks for Awardee:

  • Best Student Presenter Certificate
  • Memento / Medal / Trophy
  • Special feature in the conference newsletter
  • Discounted or complimentary registration for ICINSA-2027 (Student Category)
  • Potential nomination for Young Researcher Award in future editions

All winners will receive a certificate and a memento during the valedictory session.
