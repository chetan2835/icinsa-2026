ICINSA-2026

ICINSA-2026: The International Conference on “Innovation in Nonlinear Science and Its Applications” aims to bring together researchers, academicians, scientists, industry professionals, and students from across the globe to discuss recent advances, emerging trends, and innovative applications in the broad field of nonlinear science.

Nonlinear science plays a crucial role in understanding complex systems encountered in mathematics, physics, engineering, biology, economics, data science, and interdisciplinary domains. Rapid developments in computational methods, modeling techniques, and experimental approaches have expanded the scope and impact of nonlinear research, making it a vital area for scientific and technological progress.

ICINSA–2026 will provide an international platform for the exchange of ideas, presentation of original research findings, and fostering of collaborations among participants. The conference will feature keynote lectures by eminent experts, invited talks, technical paper presentations, and interactive discussions focused on both theoretical developments and real-world applications of nonlinear systems.

The conference is jointly organized by Anand International College of Engineering, Jaipur, and Rajasthan Ganita Parishad, with the objective of promoting research culture, encouraging innovation, and strengthening the connection between academia and industry in the domain of nonlinear science.
