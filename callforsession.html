Call for Special Session Proposals – ICINSA-2026

The Organizing Committee of ICINSA-2026 – International Conference on Innovation in Nonlinear Science and Its Applications warmly invites proposals for Special Sessions that align with the themes, scope, and objectives of the conference.

Special Sessions are intended to provide focused, in-depth discussions on emerging research areas, interdisciplinary topics, and recent advancements in nonlinear science and its diverse applications. These sessions aim to bring together leading researchers, academicians, industry experts, and practitioners to exchange ideas, share innovative methodologies, and foster meaningful collaborations.

Objectives of Special Sessions

  • Highlight cutting-edge research trends and novel applications in nonlinear science
  • Encourage interdisciplinary interaction across science, engineering, and applied domains
  • Promote collaborative research and networking among national and international participants
  • Provide a platform for focused discussion beyond regular conference tracks

Topics of Interest

  • Nonlinear dynamics and chaos theory
  • Complex systems and networks
  • Nonlinear control and optimization
  • Mathematical modeling and simulations
  • Nonlinear signal and image processing
  • Applications in engineering, physics, biology, economics, and social systems
  • Data-driven and AI-based nonlinear approaches
  • Emerging interdisciplinary applications of nonlinear science

Guidelines for Special Sessions

  • Each Special Session must include a minimum of ten (10) paper presentations.
  • The Session Organizer(s) will be responsible for:
    • Promoting the session and attracting high-quality submissions.
    • Coordinating the review of abstracts/papers in full compliance with ICINSA-2026 review standards.
  • Session organizers may serve as Chair or Co-Chair of their session.
  • A registration fee waiver for one paper will be granted to the session organizer if the session successfully registers at least ten (10) papers.

Guidelines for Special Session Proposals

Each proposal should include:

  • Title of the Special Session
  • Brief description outlining the session’s relevance and objectives
  • List of topics to be covered
  • Names, affiliations, and contact details of the Session Organizer(s)
  • Short bio of the Session Organizer(s) highlighting expertise in the proposed area
